South Asia Regional Micro Database (SARMD) User Guidelines

Introduction to metadata analysis

The second part of this book studies the available metadata for household surveys in SARMD. Metadata is documentation that describes data and may take many different forms. Chapter 4 focuses on survey availability to present precisely which surveys are available where and when. Chapter 5 goes deeper into the survey questionnaires to compare what information is collected by the surveys in relation to the four main expenditure components: food, non-food, durables, and rent.
